## How to Launch the Bot

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Go to your bot and click **Edit**.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Click the **Test** button from the top bar.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    A side drawer labeled **Preview** will appear.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Click **Copy Embed Code**.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Paste this code into the \<head> section of your website.
  </Step>
</Steps>

This renders your chatbot exactly as seen in the preview window.

## Live Customization Options

You can customize your chatbot widget from the **Customize** tab:

- **Bot Name** – Displayed on preview and widget
- **Chatbot Preview Title & Description** – For better context on your site
- **AI Agent Avatar** – Choose from available agents like Emily, Alex, Michelle, etc.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/image-1.png" />
</Frame>

- **Upload Custom Agent Photo** – JPG/PNG up to 5MB
- **Theme Color** – Set primary color of chat UI (hex supported)
- **Auto Open Toggle** – Auto-launch the widget on page load
- **Widget Position** – Choose bottom-left or bottom-right
